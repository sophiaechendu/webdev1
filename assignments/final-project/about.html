About

I am currently a Social Media and Digital Strategy Major with a minor in acting. For the past six months, I have been working on a tv show for the CW. I play the role of Mariah Kaminski, a 16 year old who just wants to do right by those she loves. Acting has always been an interest to me, as well as other areas of art. in high school I was a visual artist with a focus in drawing and painting. My favorite medium to use was Prismacolor colored pencils. I haven't maed too many new pieces, but I hope to focus on my visual art again come 2022.
